Institution Economics

2021 summer term
Notes on the lecture given by Prof. Dr. Eva Hoppe-Fischer.

Overview

This lecture can be seen as a more application aspect of contract theory. It studies different institutions with respect to the behaviour they create, with a particular focus on principle-agent model with asymmetric information.
The content consists of four chapters: Moral Hazard, Adverse Selection, The Hold-up Problem and Incomplete Contracts.
